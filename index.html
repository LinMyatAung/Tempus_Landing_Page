TempusAI and Global Health Initiative Announce "Neuro-Link" Partnership

TempusAI is proud to announce a multi-year partnership with the Global Health Initiative (GHI) to integrate our Nerve-Sync technology into prosthetic development. By utilizing synthetic neural networks, the partnership aims to restore lifelike sensory feedback to thousands of patients worldwide. This collaboration marks the first time a general-purpose AI model will be used to bridge the gap between mechanical hardware and the human nervous system in a clinical setting.

Tempus Open-Source: Releasing the "Chronos-1" Dataset to the Public

In our ongoing commitment to ethical and transparent AI, TempusAI has officially released the Chronos-1 dataset—the world’s most comprehensive collection of subatomic physical simulations. This release allows researchers and students globally to access high-fidelity data previously locked behind enterprise-grade quantum hardware. We believe that by democratizing access to these powerful tools, we can accelerate the pace of global scientific discovery together.
